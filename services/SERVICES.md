## How Pricing Works

### Factors Affecting Cost

- **Project complexity** — More complex projects cost more
- **Scope and timeline** — Larger scope or urgent timelines may increase cost
- **Expertise required** — Specialized expertise may cost more
- **Number of revisions** — Extra revisions beyond package inclusions
- **Ongoing vs. one-time** — Retainers offer discounted rates
- **Industry or regulatory requirements** — Some industries require specialized knowledge

### How We Determine Price

1. **Initial consultation** — Understand your scope and requirements
2. **Detailed proposal** — Clear timeline, deliverables, and pricing
3. **Transparent communication** — Costs are clear before starting
4. **Change management** — Discussion if scope changes

### Payment Terms

- **50% deposit** upon agreement
- **50% upon completion** of deliverables
- **Monthly invoicing** for retainer arrangements
- **Flexible options** available for longer projects

### Discounts Available

- **Volume discounts** for multiple projects
- **Long-term discounts** for ongoing support arrangements
- **Package deals** for bundled services
- **Non-profit rates** may be available
