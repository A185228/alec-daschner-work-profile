# Templates & Reusable Resources

This folder contains reusable templates, frameworks, checklists, and resources for writing, documentation, safety, risk management, compliance, and administrative support.

## Folder Structure

```
templates/
├── writing-documentation/      # Writing templates and style guides
├── safety-risk-management/     # Risk and safety templates
├── compliance-standards/       # Compliance and audit templates
├── admin-support/              # Administrative templates
└── general/                    # Cross-functional templates
```

## Available Templates

### Writing & Documentation
- Policy and procedure templates
- Style guides and writing checklists
- Document templates (reports, proposals, etc.)
- Technical writing templates

### Safety & Risk Management
- Risk assessment templates
- Safety protocol templates
- Incident report templates
- Hazard identification checklists

### Compliance & Standards
- Compliance framework templates
- Audit checklists
- Standards mapping templates
- Compliance tracker templates

### Administrative Support
- Meeting coordination templates
- Project tracking templates
- Status report templates
- Administrative checklists

## Using These Templates

All templates are provided as starting points and should be adapted to your specific needs, industry, and context.

**How to use:**
1. Select the template that best matches your need
2. Download or copy the template
3. Adapt headings, sections, and content to your context
4. Customize as needed for your organization
