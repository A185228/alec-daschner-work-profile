3. **Compliance & Standards** — Compliance frameworks, audit preparation, regulatory documentation, standards implementation
4. **Remote Administrative Support** — Project coordination, process optimization, administrative documentation, workflow improvement

Yes, I work with:

You might benefit from my services if:

## Working with Me

### How do we get started?

1. **Initial Contact** — You describe your project or need
2. **Discovery Conversation** — We discuss scope, timeline, requirements, and budget
3. **Proposal** — I provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing
4. **Agreement** — We finalize terms and begin work
5. **Delivery** — Work is completed on schedule with revision support and regular updates

### What if I'm not sure about the scope?

That's completely normal. We can:
- Start with a consultation to clarify your needs
- Begin with a smaller pilot or discovery project
- Adjust scope as we better understand the work
- Work iteratively with regular check-ins
- Build understanding together

Typically:

- I'm transparent about revision expectations upfront

### Do you have a contract?

Yes, all projects include a clear agreement that outlines:
- Project scope and deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- Pricing and payment terms
- Revision policy
- Confidentiality agreements
- Next steps and process

The agreement is simple and clear, not overly complex.

### What's your cancellation or refund policy?

Cancellation and refund policies:
- If you cancel before work begins, the deposit is refunded (minus reasonable admin costs if applicable)
- If you cancel during the project, we discuss a fair outcome
- If you're unsatisfied with deliverables, we work to resolve or discuss alternatives
- The goal is a fair, reasonable outcome for both parties

These details are clarified in the agreement.

### How do you handle scope changes?

Scope changes:
- Are discussed upfront during the proposal phase
- Can be managed if they occur during the project
- May affect timeline or pricing
- Are communicated clearly and transparently
- Require mutual agreement

### What if there are issues or concerns?

If issues arise:
- We communicate openly and directly
- We work together to find a solution
- The goal is mutual satisfaction
- Problems are addressed promptly
- We maintain a professional, collaborative approach
